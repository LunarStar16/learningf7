Fall Recommendations

Things to watch for the spooky fall season

Hocus Pocus
A classic movie about witches coming back from the dead due to a virgin lighting a black flame cnadle and wreaking havoc on the town of Salem. A great witchy film to start off fall.
The Chilling Adventures of Sabrina
A dark take on 'sabrina the teenage witch' from the archie comics, a great series to begin watching in the spooky fall season.
Witches of East End
A show about witches cursed and exiled from their homeland, struggling to survive in the mortal world, a great witchy show for fall, a great book too if you prefer reading.
Casper
A friendly ghost named Casper to accompany you in the fall season, about a family moving into a haunted home and befriending a ghost named Casper and trying to survive the scares of Caspers family.
Buffy the Vampire Slayer
A show to fill youre fall vampire needs, Buffy came to slay... vampires and other various monsters.
The School for Good and Evil
A film about how the fairytales we've read are real and how the heros and villains of those stories are trained at this school for good and evil. it's not yet released but the trailer looks stunning and already has me excited to watch and even read the books.
